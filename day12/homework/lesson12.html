Saved vs Linked Image

Saved Image

A saved image loads faster and is always available, even without an internet connection.

Additionally, you have full control over the image, and it won't disappear.

Linked Image

A linked image depends on an external website. If the website removes the image, it will no longer will appear.

However, it doesn't take up storage space on your own server.
